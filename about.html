About Mkeet

MKEET-KU Which stands for Mt Kenya East Evagelistic Team is an evagelistic team from Kenyatta University.
It is a team of kenyatta university student who have joined together to serve God in ministry. Preaching the gospel of truth to all nation.

Mission

  1. holding joint mision and joint fellowship
  2. teaching divine truth

Vision

To develop a wide network of Evangelism and nurturing members to ensure the unification of the body of christ
